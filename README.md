## 📌 Project Overview

This project demonstrates an **industry-level Business Intelligence solution** built using **PostgreSQL** and **Microsoft Power BI**.

The project transforms raw Olist E-Commerce data into an optimized analytical model using SQL and presents business insights through interactive Power BI dashboards.

# 📊 Key Business Insights

The dashboard uncovers several actionable insights across sales, customer behavior, logistics, and seller performance:

### 📈 Sales Performance
- **Revenue increased by 18.4% Year-over-Year (YoY)**, indicating consistent business growth compared to the previous year.
- **The Top 5 product categories generated 62% of total revenue**, highlighting a strong concentration of sales and opportunities for focused inventory and marketing strategies.

### 🌍 Customer Insights
- **São Paulo contributed the highest customer base and overall revenue**, making it the most valuable market for customer acquisition and retention initiatives.

### 🚚 Delivery & Logistics
- **92% of delivered orders were completed on time**, demonstrating an efficient logistics network while identifying the remaining delayed deliveries as an area for operational improvement.

### ⭐ Customer Reviews
- **84% of all rated orders received positive reviews (4–5 stars)**, reflecting high customer satisfaction and strong product/service quality.

### 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus promotional campaigns on the highest-performing product categories while exploring growth opportunities in underperforming categories.
- Strengthen customer engagement in high-value regions like São Paulo and replicate successful strategies in other states.
- Investigate the root causes of delayed deliveries to further improve customer satisfaction.
- Leverage positive customer feedback in marketing campaigns to build trust and improve conversion rates.
- Monitor seller performance and incentivize top-performing sellers to maintain service quality and revenue growth.
